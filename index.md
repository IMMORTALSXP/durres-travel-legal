## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how VRANA ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Durres Travel Guide mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

**Location Data**  
The App may access your device location **while the App is open** to provide maps, navigation, and directions to bus stations and other highlighted points of interest. Location data is **not collected in the background**.

**Usage Data**  
The App may collect anonymous usage statistics, such as which features are used and app performance, to improve reliability and user experience.

**Preferences**  
User preferences and settings are stored **locally on your device**.

---

## 3. Use of Maps and Open APIs

The App uses a **third-party map service** to display maps and navigation features.

Points of interest, bus stations, and locations highlighted on the map are **predefined by the App** and are **not user-generated**.

Requests to these APIs may be processed by the respective service providers according to their **own privacy policies**. VRANA does not control or store data processed by these external services.

---

## 4. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

- Provide app functionality  
- Enable location-based navigation features  
- Improve app performance and stability  
- Diagnose and fix technical issues

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We **do not sell, rent, or share personal data**. Data may be transmitted to third-party services only as required to provide core app functionality (such as maps or analytics).

---

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information used by the App. However, **no system is completely secure**, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 7. User Control

You may disable location permissions at any time through your device settings. You may also **uninstall the App** to stop all data collection.

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

The App is **not intended for children under the age of 13**, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

---

## 9. Future Analytics

VRANA may collect **anonymous usage data in the future** to understand how the App is used, improve features, and fix issues. This data will **not include personal information** and will be used solely to enhance app performance and user experience.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be **reflected within the App**.
